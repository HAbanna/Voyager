Jhau Bon at Kuakat : Beautiful Jhau Bon or Jhau forest is situated at kuakata. Jhau Bon is another nice spot of kuakata. This forest surrounded by many jhau and coconut trees. It's full of natural beauty. Jhau bon is near to the beach. You can visit there by walking or bike. In jhau bon or jhau forest there is a Ecko park. Ecko park of kuakata is very beautiful spot  for made a picnic. Early in the morning the sun rising can be viewed from the corner.
